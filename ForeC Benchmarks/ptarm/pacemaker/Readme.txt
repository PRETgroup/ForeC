Author: Eugene Kin Chee Yip (27 September 2013)

Prototype implementation of a pacemaker.

Files:
* pacemaker_functional.pdf: High-level abstraction of the pacemaker.

Folders:
* forec: Synchronous implementation of just the life critical tasks.
* forecast: Synchronous implementation with life, mission and non-critical tasks.
* malardalen: WCET benchmarks that are used for the mission and non-critical tasks.
* uppaal: Model of a pacemaker for the life critical tasks.
